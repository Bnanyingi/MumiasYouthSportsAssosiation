Employability and work readiness

The MYSA SC work readiness is a programm that seeks to help the youth of Mumias to acquire necessary skills and knowledge needed to acces formal employement majority of the MYSA SC youth depend on football to earn a stable living and do not have such skill
The programm has two targeted groups which are youth between the ages of 16 - 26 and children between the age of 10 - 15 for those transitting to or through high school
